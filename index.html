Butter Chicken

Non-Veg

Tender chicken in a rich, creamy tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken thighs
  • 2 cups Tomato purée
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Garam Masala

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Cook chicken in a pan until browned.
  3. Simmer tomato purée with butter and spices.
  4. Add chicken and cream, simmer for 10 mins.

Palak Paneer

Vegetarian

Fresh cottage cheese cubes in a smooth spinach gravy.

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer cubes
  • 2 bunches Spinach (blanched)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds

Instructions

  1. Blend blanched spinach into a paste.
  2. Sauté cumin, onions, and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add spinach puree and spices. Cook for 5 mins.
  4. Gently fold in paneer cubes and serve hot.

Masala Chai

Beverage

Spiced tea brewed with milk and aromatic herbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 tsp Tea powder
  • 1 inch Ginger (crushed)
  • 2 Cardamom pods

Instructions

  1. Boil water with ginger and cardamom.
  2. Add tea powder and boil for 2 mins.
  3. Add milk and sugar to taste.
  4. Strain and serve hot.
